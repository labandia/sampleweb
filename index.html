R3LAMP features

Faculty Panel

As a faculty, one will be more capable to utilize the features of R3LAMP. These include the supervisory control over the students and the following:

  • Integration of Some GSuite Tools and Services
  • Create and manage classes
  • Upload Learning Resources and Activities
  • Posting of Announcement and Comments
  • Messaging
  • Schedule Synchronous/Online Classes

Frequently Asked Questions ( FAQs )

Is R3LAMP Free?

Yes. R3LAMP will be available for free for all HEIs who might need it. R3LAMP is one of the projects under the Central Luzon Regional Education Action Plan of CHEDR3 and funded under the Grant-in-Aid program of DOST3.

What are the hardware/software requirements of R3LAMP?

YR3LAMP will run in any latest browser available. It does not require high-end hardware to run the application

How do we set up R3LAMP in our institution?

3LAMP needs to be installed on the institution's hosting site. The hosting site should have at least PHP version 7.4 and above. Setting up the database, backend, and frontend shall be done by personnel with knowledge in web management.

Will there be users' training before implementation?

Yes, part of the Project Timeline is the User's Training. The following set of training is provided:

  1. Installation of the application.
  2. Use of different phases.
  3. Troubleshooting and management.
How will our enrollment system be connected to R3LAMP?

R3LAMP works as a stand-alone application. It can't be integrated with the institutions' enrollment system. But we have provided a feature where the institution may upload a master list of classes, faculty, and enrolled students. Templates are also provided for reference.
